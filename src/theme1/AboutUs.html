About Us

We began as a high-end interiors contractor. We are today, an interdisciplinary architectural studio with cultural, residential and commercial projects built worldwide.

Incorporated on 06-AUG-2019, Consilio Rabbani LLP is based out of our centrally located Bengaluru office. We are extremely passionate about what we do and our quality standards. Our highly innovative, experienced and reliable team help bring your concepts and ideas to life. Use of modern technology, industry standards, timely and quality deliveries, our experienced workforce, and our constant efforts in improving our processes and quality to better serve our customers, are our USPs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a leading MCA Provider, providing superior quality products and services at competitive prices. We want be a globally innovative and competitive business, providing 100% genuine services to our customers. We are committed to total customer satisfaction by providing quality products & services.

As you might expect of a company that began as a high-end interiors contractor, we pay strict attention to detail, ensure value-for-money without compromising on quality, and ensure premium quality by opting for branded and long-lasting materials.

Our Vision

Our commitment to exceptional quality has never wavered. Today we rank amongst some of the most highly-regarded construction and interior designers. We are proud to be the preferred partners to some of reputed builders, providing home, retail and office interiors.

Why Choose Us?

We help you discover your design taste.

We then provide creative design options - functional and aesthetic, to suit your needs and likes.

Our team of experienced design specialists, help you choose the best materials and finishes for your space.

We provide 3D sketches and render virtual surface images, including walk-throughs, to give you a more clearer understanding, before starting the manufacturing works.

Understanding your budget needs, our team help ensure that we provide you the best value your money can buy without compromising on quality.

By using superior quality and branded materials, we ensure that we are able to provide premium services to all of our clients.

Our controlled and constantly monitored manufacturing process, ensures fast execution and on-time completion of all our projects.

Customer Satisfaction is our top most priority. We are more than willing to go out of our way to help ensure we do not miss out on any of your needs, however small it may be.
